Wisdom Warriors




A New Kind of Education for a New Generation



Wisdom Warriors is a New Educational Game for a New Era of Students.

Wisdom Warriors is an educational video game for 1st Grade Students currently available for the Web on most mobile devices and computers. The game employs 1st grade level education styles and standards in collaboration with US Department of Education Common Core Standards to align the game's curriculum with that of the student's class. Specifically, Wisdom Warriors focusses on the core subjects of:

  • English Language Arts
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies (History & Geography)



Wisdom Warriors uses principles of Gamification to engage students in their learning and motiviate them to succeed.

The Game's mission-based structure allows students to choose their own path through the game and allows them to play at their own speed and level of learning. For each mission, students choose 1 of the 4 Common Core Subjects and use knowledge and skills related to that subject to solve a challenge. Problem-Solving challenges include solving a puzzle, analyzing a letter for clues, inventing a machine, solving a riddle, and more. It's up to the student to choose how they want to solve the problem.

Upon successful completion of the challenge, students are rewarded with Experience Points (XP) based on how well they solve the problem. And to encourage students to use a wide varierty of problem-solving methods and tactics, students are rewarded for improving their skills across all 4 Subjects, especially the student's weaker Subjects.



Bilingual Learning

In order to broaden access to primary education (especially for Hispanic and low-income students and families) through this game, Wisdom Warriors is entirely bilingual. That means students can play every level and every Problem-Solving Challenge in either English or Spanish. Students having trouble with a certain level in English can quickly play the same level in Spanish in order to better understand what the aim of the challenge is (and vice verse). This feature makes Wisdom Warriors especially suitable for International Schools or biigual classes, especially with a student population of varying levels of Spanish or English proficiency.






More than just a Character

With 8 diverse Characters each with their own unique appearances, warrior types and learning styles, every student is sure to find a character they can personally identify with and play as. The game's wide array of characters promotes diversity in gender, race, class, sexual orientation, learning disability, physique and Problem-Solving abilities.





Stay in the Loop

Downloadble Progress Reports about your students' allow you to keep up to date with how your students are advancing through the game. Track each student's daily activity on the game with data on daily logins, play time, character information, progression through the game, Experience Points (XP), question answers, and strengths and weaknesses. You can use these progress reports to better tailor your class' curriculum around your students in order for them to get the most out of their education.




Going beyond Video Games

Go beyond the game with custom classroom group activities in line with the plot and activities of the game. Extension activities include group discussions, team challenges and group games that facilitate social and cognitive reasoning skills among the students. Group Activity topcis range from discussions on diversity to group social teambuilding exercises based off of accomplishments from the game.
